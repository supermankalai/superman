Advanced Kafka Concepts

Advanced Kafka topics including partitioning strategies, consumer groups, exactly-once semantics, and performance optimization.

Partitioning Strategies

Effective partitioning is crucial for Kafka performance and scalability. Different strategies serve different use cases:

Key-based Partitioning:

  • Ensures ordering for related messages
  • Ideal for stateful processing
  • Requires careful key selection

Round-robin Partitioning:

  • Even distribution across partitions
  • Good for stateless processing
    • <极li>No ordering guarantees

Consumer Groups and Rebalancing

Rebalancing Strategies:

  • Eager rebalancing for immediate redistribution
  • Incremental cooperative rebalancing for minimal disruption
  • Static membership for stable consumer assignments

Exactly-Once Semantics

Kafka provides transactional guarantees for exactly-once processing:

Transactional Producers:

  • Enable.idempotence=true for deduplication
  • Transactional.id for cross-partition atomicity
  • Commit and abort semantics

Transactional Consumers:

  • Read committed messages only
  • Isolation level configuration
  • Offset management within transactions
