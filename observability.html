The Observatory

Building comprehensive observability for data pipelines. Metrics, logging, tracing, and alerting strategies for distributed streaming systems.

What is Observability?

Observability is the ability to understand the internal state of a system by examining its outputs. It goes beyond traditional monitoring by providing deeper insights into system behavior and enabling faster problem resolution.

Key Pillars of Observability

  • Metrics: Quantitative data about system performance
  • Logs: Detailed records of system events
  • Tracing: End-to-end tracking of requests across services
  • Alerting: Automated notifications for critical issues

Benefits of Observability

Observability provides:

  • Faster problem detection and resolution
  • Better understanding of system behavior
  • Improved performance optimization
  • Enhanced user experience
